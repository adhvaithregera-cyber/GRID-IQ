Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 2026

GridIQ ("we", "us") is an unofficial fan app for the 2026 motorsport season. This policy explains what data we collect, how we use it, and how you can control it.

What we collect

  • Account info — when you sign in with Google or email, we receive your name, email address, and profile photo from your provider.
  • Subscription status — whether you hold an active GridIQ Pro subscription (stored in our database).
  • Usage analytics — page views and feature interactions via Google Analytics (anonymous, no personal identifiers).
  • Last seen date — the last time you signed in, stored in our database.

What we do NOT collect

  • Payment card numbers or UPI details — payments go directly through your UPI app and never pass through our servers.
  • Location data.
  • Device identifiers beyond what Firebase Analytics collects by default.

How we use your data

  • To identify your account and save your preferences across devices.
  • To verify your Pro subscription status.
  • To respond to payment verification emails you send us.
  • To improve the app using anonymous analytics data.

Third-party services

  • Firebase (Google) — authentication, database, and analytics. Firebase Privacy.
  • Google Analytics — anonymous usage tracking. You can opt out via Google Analytics Opt-out.
  • QR Server (goqr.me) — generates the UPI QR code displayed in the Pro modal. No personal data is sent.

Data retention

Your account data is retained while your account is active. You can request deletion at any time by emailing us and we will remove your record within 7 days.

Your rights

You can request a copy of, or deletion of, any personal data we hold about you by contacting us at the address below.

Children

GridIQ is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect data from children under 13.

Contact

Questions or requests: gridiq.app@gmail.com
